协会宗旨

1: 北域华人协会作为一个社会窗口，让北域民众了介我们华人社团乃至中国新风貌， 分享华人社团和中国实事和故事，使华人社团得到更好的理介和尊重。 促进多元化民族社会发展。

2: 协会作为政府政策传导和沟通桥梁，能更有效反馈华人自身祈求和意见， 保护华人基本权益，也是政府政策信息传递，翻译和剖介的中介。

3: 为新移民和移居北地华人提供实用信息，和工作， 学习和生意上指导，为受困的华人提供实际救助和帮助。

4: 通过承办中国传统节日活动和中文学校课程，使海外华人和小孩， 既作为新西兰有义务的好公民，又能认同和传承中国优秀传统文化， 也是宣扬中国文化主要途径，促进多民族之间相互了介， 尊重和欣赏，创造多民族合谐社会。

5: 积极倡导奉献社会义工活动，和促进身心健康的体育活动， 个人和社会双受益，创造美好生活家园。

Association purpose

1: The Northern Territory Chinese Association serves as a social window, allowing the people of the Northern Territory to introduce our Chinese communities and even the new style of China, share Chinese communities and Chinese facts and stories, so that the Chinese communities can be better introduced and respected. Promote the social development of diversified nationalities.

2: As a government policy transmission and communication bridge, the association can more effectively feed back the Chinese people's own prayers and opinions, protect the basic rights and interests of the Chinese people, and is also an intermediary for government policy information transmission, translation and analysis.

3: Provide practical information for new immigrants and Chinese immigrants in Northland, as well as guidance on work, study and business, and provide practical relief and help to the Chinese in difficulties.

4: By undertaking Chinese traditional festival activities and Chinese school courses, overseas Chinese and children will not only be good citizens of New Zealand, but also recognize and inherit the excellent traditional Chinese culture. It is also the main way to promote Chinese culture and promote multi-ethnic relations. Introduce each other, respect and appreciate each other, and create a multi-ethnic harmonious society.

5: Actively advocate dedication to social volunteer activities, and sports activities that promote physical and mental health, benefit both individuals and society, and create a better home for life.
